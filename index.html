About Me

I am a Ph.D. student passionate about teaching and making complex scientific concepts accessible. My research focuses on cosmic ray muons and atmospheric effects, particularly high-energy cosmic ray interactions. I also develop advanced data acquisition systems using Raspberry Pi and Arduino and have expertise in Python and ROOT CERN for statistical data analysis.
